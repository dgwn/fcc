Ludwig Wittgenstein

Philosopher of Language

Portrait of Wittgenstein on being awarded a scholarship from Trinity College, Cambridge, 1929.

Here are some important events with regard to Wittgenstein's life:

  • April 26. 1889 - Born in Vienna, Austria-Hungary
  • October 23, 1906 - Began studies in mechanical engineering
  • October 18, 1911 - Wittgenstein meets Bertrand Russell
  • March 1916 - Began fighting for the Austro-Hungarian army
  • August 1918 - Finished work on Tractatus Logico-Philosophicus
  • 1933 - 1935 - Lectures which make up the Blue and Brown Books were given
  • April 29, 1951 - Ludwig Wittgenstein died in Cambridge, England
  • 1953 - Philosophical Investigations was published
